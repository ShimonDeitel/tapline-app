Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-08

Tapline - Beer Log is an offline-first app. All data you enter (beer, brewery, style, rating) is stored locally on your device in the app's private storage. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. The app makes no network requests except standard StoreKit calls to Apple's servers to process optional Pro purchases.

We do not use analytics, advertising identifiers, or third-party trackers.

If you delete the app, your locally stored data is removed with it.
